About Us


Our Unique Food Cart: Burrito Masala!!


“I am living the American Dream. My business partner (Jose) and I both worked in the restaurant business in our native land of Mexico and India respectively. Portland’s downtown Food Cart business have provided us economic opportunities that we could only dream of. This dream is now a reality.! Culinary experimentation for Portland’s culturally diverse population has been an exciting venture. Living in an organic state like Oregon, we were happy to incorporate the “ farm-to-table “concept of outdoor dining for local Audiences and Tourists alike.


Our uniqueness is the fusion of mouthwatering exotic ingredients that our Audience can truly experiment with and “have it their way!”. Serving organic and quality ingredients with continuous family friendly service gives us a competitive advantage. We always strive to make creative changes to our menu ingredients to keep our Portland Audience Excited to always come back for more!”
